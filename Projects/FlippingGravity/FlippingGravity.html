Flipping Gravity

Flipping Gravity is a game me and my cousin made when I first started learning Unity and C#.

We were 11 years old at that time.

My cousin did the graphics and I did the coding.

We released it on the play store but after long inactivity the account was shut down and the game isn't available anymore.

Flipping Gravity is a puzzle game where you rotate you screen to change the gravity and reach the rocket to finish.
