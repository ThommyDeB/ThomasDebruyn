Born a digital native

My journey began on June 21, 2009. A date that marks the start of a life interwined with technology and innovation.

Codefever

Inspired by my family, I took my first steps into programming with Codefever. It wasn't just a course; it was the ignition of a lifelong passion for coding.

Anonymous

I embraced the challenge of cybersecurity with Anonymous, a Codefever initiative that demystified the art of ethical hacking.

C# and Unity

With my father's guidance, I delved into the realms of game development. Learning Unity and C# became more than a hobby; it was a craft I was determined to master.

Tower defense game

My ambition materialized into a tower defense game. A project started with youthful enthusiasm and completed with the finesse of experience.

BeCP

My competitive spirit shone at the Belgian Olympiad in Informatics (BeOI), where I secured 3rd place. This triumph led me to the Belgian Competitive Programming (BeCP) team, and later to represent my skills at the European Junior Olympiad in Informatics (EJOI).

HTML CSS Javascript

At the beginning of 2024, I embarked on an exciting journey to learn HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. These foundational languages have empowered me to create beautiful and interactive web experiences.
